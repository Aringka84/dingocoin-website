DingoTip

Introducing DingoTip - the hassle-free app that lets you tip or pay anyone using just their phone number or email! Whether you want to show appreciation for great service, strike up a conversation or simply transfer value, DingoTip makes it super easy to do so.

With DingoTip, you can even send Dingocoin to new users, who will be notified via phone or email. And if they don't sign up, the Dingocoin will be safely returned to you. Plus, DingoTip is non-custodial, which means you are in complete control of the Dingocoin you own.

Initially, DingoTip will support Dingocoin exclusively, with plans to introduce support for Dogecoin in the near future, along with a feature to exchange between the two. Looking ahead, we also plan to enable Fiat deposits and Point of Sale integration, so you can enjoy even more flexibility and convenience.

Get started with DingoTip today and experience the future of effortless tipping and payments!
